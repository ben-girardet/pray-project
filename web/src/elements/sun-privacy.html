Privacy Policy

Sunago utilizes state-of-the-art security and end-to-end encryption to provide a secure service when it comes to storing and sharing data (prayer topics and messages). Your data are always encrypted, so they can never be shared or viewed by anyone but yourself and the intended recipients.

Information you provide

Account information. You register a phone number, along with your firstname. You may optionally add other informations in your account such as a profile picture. After the Beta phase, these data will be end-to-end encrypted1.

Prayer topics. When you create a prayer topic, it is encrypted with a singular key and all its content cannot be read without your intentional sharing1.

Shared data. When you share a prayer topic to another user or a group of user, you give each of the recipient to read the topic and all related messages. They will receive the topic crypting key in the form of an asymetric key that is only readable by a specific recipient.

User support. If you contact Sunago with a support requets or any inquery, any personal data you may share with us is kept only for the purposes of researching the issue and contacting you about your case.

Analytics. We keep some statistical data in order to learn how people use the app and improve important features. The kind of informations we retain for this purpose are always anonymous and only includes number (no text or image content). For exemple we track the number of topics created, the number of prayers logged or number of messages written on average by user or by topic.

Information we may share

Third parties. We work with third parties to provide some of our Services. For example, our Third-Party Providers send a verification code to your phone number when you register for our Services. These providers are bound by their Privacy Policies to safeguard that information. If you use other Third-Party Services like YouTube, Spotify, Giphy, etc. in connection with our Services, their Terms and Privacy Policies govern your use of those services.

Other instances where Signal may need to share your data

  • To meet any applicable law, regulation, legal process or enforceable governmental request.
  • To enforce applicable Terms, including investigation of potential violations.
  • To detect, prevent, or otherwise address fraud, security, or technical issues.
  • To protect against harm to the rights, property, or safety of Sunago, our users, or the public as required or permitted by law.

Updates

We will update this privacy policy as needed so that it is current, accurate, and as clear as possible. Your continued use of our Services confirms your acceptance of our updated Privacy Policy.
