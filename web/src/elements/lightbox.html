Your data belongs to you

Privacy by design*

While developing Sunago we make everything we can to keep your data on your devices and not on our servers.

  • We only keep on our servers the minimum data required to make the application work
  • Your identity and all your content is end-to-end encrypted
  • You can download a copy of your data at any time
  • You can edit and delete anything and once you do, it will be gone.
  • Quick formatting tools
  • Fast email checking

*We are currently in our beta phase. It means that some of these features are still being developed. In order to provide a great user experience and quickly learn from user feedback, during the beta phase we tend to keep more data that we'll do in the long end. When you agree to be part of the beta program you agree that some of your data are kept on our servers and not all the data is end-to-end encrypted yet.
