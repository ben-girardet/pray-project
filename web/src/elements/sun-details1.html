Privacy by design*

While developing Sunago we make everything we can to keep your data on your devices and not on our servers.

  • We only keep on our servers the minimum data required to make the application work
  • Your identity and all your content is end-to-end encrypted
  • You can download a copy of your data at any time
  • You can edit and delete anything and once you do, it will be gone.
  • Quick formatting tools
  • Fast email checking
